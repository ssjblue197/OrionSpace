Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. This document clearly explains what we collect, why, and what rights you have over your data.

Last updated: July 6, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Orino Studio collects the minimum information necessary to provide the service:

  • Account information: your email address when you sign up via email or Google.
  • Payment information: transactions are processed by third-party payment gateways. We do not store bank card details.
  • Service operation data: signed-in devices, daily feature usage counts (numbers only, never your content), and application version — used to enforce plan limits, manage devices, and deliver automatic updates.
Videos, audio, voice-clone samples, and content you process in the app are never sent to our servers. All AI processing happens locally on your computer.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used to:

  • Authenticate your identity and manage login sessions.
  • Verify subscription status and device limits.
  • Send registration confirmation emails, account notifications, and invoices.
  • Deliver automatic application updates.
  • Provide technical support when you request it.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Account information is stored on secure infrastructure: passwords are always encrypted before storage, all connections are protected with HTTPS, and login sessions expire and can be remotely revoked when needed.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The orino.space website uses only the minimal technical cookies required for basic operation. We do not use analytics cookies, behavioral tracking, or third-party advertising.

The desktop application stores session tokens locally on your device. These tokens are never shared with any third party.

When you use the browser-cookie feature to download login-gated content, cookies are read and used locally on your machine to authenticate with the source platform — they are never sent to Orino Studio's servers.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectify: update your account information from within the app or via support email.
  • Delete: request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Unsubscribe: opt out of email communications by contacting support.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via the Contact page or email support@orino.space.

6. Third-Party Services

Orino Studio integrates with the following services, each with its own privacy policy:

  • PayOS: handles Visa/Mastercard payment processing. We never receive or store card data.
  • PayPal: handles PayPal (USD) payments. Transactions take place on PayPal's systems.
  • OpenAI / Anthropic (optional): if you choose to use an external AI provider for translation, subtitle text will be sent to that provider. This data does not pass through Orino's servers.

7. User-Generated Content

Everything you process and create with Orino Studio (translated videos, dubs, synthesized or cloned voices) is produced and stored locally on your machine — we do not access, moderate, or store this content.

You are therefore solely responsible for the content you produce and for how you use, publish, and distribute it. The publisher is not responsible for content created by users with the application. See Terms of Use — section 7 for the full obligations and disclaimer.

8. Policy Changes

If we make significant changes, we will notify you via your registered email or an in-app notification at least 7 days before the changes take effect. The last-updated date is always shown at the top of this page.
