FAQ

Common questions

What is Orino Studio?
An AI video processing app that runs entirely on your computer: download videos, auto-translate & dub them, and create AI voices — without uploading anything to any server.
Do I need internet or a GPU?
After setup, most features run fully offline. An NVIDIA GPU makes processing faster, but CPU-only machines run every feature — just pick the Fast dubbing engine.
Is my video data uploaded anywhere?
No. Speech recognition, translation, dubbing, and voice cloning all happen locally on your machine. We never see or store your content.
How does the free trial work?
Sign up with email or Google and get 1 day with every feature enabled — no card required. Trial exports carry a watermark and daily usage limits apply.
Can it translate videos with burned-in subtitles?
Yes. OCR reads the on-screen text, AI masks the original subtitles (inpaint / blur / dark background), and your translation renders on top with the original timing.
Which payment methods are accepted?
Visa / Mastercard and PayPal (USD). Your plan activates automatically right after payment.
