Overview
What is Orino Studio?
Orino Studio is an AI-powered video processing application that runs entirely on your computer. You can download videos from the web, automatically translate & dub them, and generate AI voice — all without uploading any data to any server.
Does Orino Studio require an internet connection?
After installation and downloading the required AI resources, most features run completely offline. An internet connection is only needed when: downloading videos from the web, using an external AI translator (OpenAI / Claude), or updating the application.
Which operating systems does Orino Studio support?
Orino Studio currently supports Windows 10/11 and macOS. A Linux version is in development.
Is my video data sent to a server?
No. All processing — speech recognition, translation, dubbing, voice synthesis — happens locally on your computer. Orino Studio does not collect or store the content of your videos.
What are the hardware requirements?
An NVIDIA GPU (CUDA) is recommended for the fastest processing, but CPU-only machines run every feature — for dubbing, pick the Fast engine for good CPU speed. Allow roughly 3–5GB of disk space for the AI models (~4.4GB for the GPU build, ~2.6GB for CPU).
I have an Intel Mac (not Apple Silicon). Can I install it?
Yes. The download page offers two separate macOS builds: Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3/M4) and Intel. Pick the one matching your chip.
Account & Plans
How does the free trial work?
Create a new account (via email or Google) and you get a free 1-day trial with full access to all features. No payment information required.
What plans are available?
Orino Studio offers monthly, 3-month, and 6-month plans. Each plan allows login on a set number of devices simultaneously. See the Pricing page for details.
Can I use it on multiple computers?
Yes, up to the device limit of your current plan. If all device slots are taken, you can sign out an old device from the Account section to sign in on a new one.
Which payment methods are accepted?
Visa / Mastercard and PayPal (USD). Your plan activates automatically right after a successful payment. All transactions go through a secure payment gateway — Orino Studio never stores your card information.
What are the trial limitations compared to paid plans?
The trial unlocks every feature but applies daily usage limits (video downloads, translations, voice generations) and all exports carry the default watermark. Upgrading to any paid plan removes these limits.
Video Download
Which platforms does Orino Studio support for downloading?
Supports YouTube, TikTok, Bilibili, Douyin, Facebook, and hundreds of other sources. You can paste a link directly or search by keyword on YouTube and Bilibili directly within the app.
Can I download multiple videos at once?
Yes. Orino Studio supports batch downloading by selecting multiple videos in search results, or by importing a .txt file containing a list of URLs (one per line). All will be queued and downloaded in parallel.
How do I download videos that require login or member-only content?
Go to the Cookie section in the Video Download screen and select the browser where you are already logged in to that platform. The app will read the authentication data and download the video normally.
Can I download only subtitles or only thumbnails?
Yes. In the download options, you can independently toggle each content type: Video, Thumbnail, Subtitles.
Can I download a whole playlist or channel?
Yes. Enable "Download playlist / channel" in the advanced options and every video in the list is queued. You can also enable SponsorBlock to auto-cut sponsor segments from YouTube videos, and embed metadata (title, uploader) into the files.
I only need a portion of a long video. How?
Switch to "Clip" mode when downloading: set the start and end timestamps and the app downloads just that segment instead of the whole video.
Translation & Dubbing
Which languages does Orino Studio support for translation?
Around 50 target languages, including Vietnamese, English, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Spanish, French, German, Russian, Thai, Indonesian, Portuguese, and many more. The source language is detected automatically.
What is the difference between offline, online, and external AI translation?
Offline translation runs entirely on your machine, needs no internet, and is free — suitable for most everyday content. Online translation (Google) is fast and light on your machine but requires internet. External AI translation (OpenAI or Claude) delivers the highest quality for specialized content and complex writing styles.
Can it translate videos with hard-coded (burned-in) subtitles?
Yes. Set the transcript source to "Hard subtitles in video" — the app reads the on-screen text with OCR, keeps the original timing, and masks the old subtitles with AI inpainting, blur, or a dark background before rendering the translated ones on top.
I only want subtitles, not dubbing. Is that possible?
Yes. In the Output section, enable only the "Subtitles" chip — the app skips voice generation and exports the video with burned-in subtitles much faster. You can also export the translated .srt on its own.
What is the difference between the Fast and Premium dubbing engines?
Fast is optimized for speed and runs well even without a GPU — great for Vietnamese/English. Premium delivers the most natural, highest-quality voice and is recommended when you have an NVIDIA GPU. You can switch engines right in the Translate screen.
Can I keep the original background music after dubbing?
Yes. Choose "Music only" — AI separates the vocals from the original audio and keeps the music, then mixes it under the new dubbed voice. Background volume is adjustable and automatically ducks under speech.
Can I export vertical 9:16 video for TikTok / Shorts?
Yes. On export, choose the 9:16 (vertical), 1:1 (square), or 4:3 aspect ratio, plus optional flip, zoom, color adjustments, and the Ken Burns effect.
I already have a subtitle file. Can I skip the speech recognition step?
Yes. Drag your .srt or .vtt file into the transcript attachment field, and the app will skip recognition and go straight to translation.
Does the dubbed audio sound natural?
Orino Studio synthesizes AI voice in multiple languages. For more natural results, use the Voice Clone feature — clone a voice from a short sample and use it for dubbing.
Can I edit subtitles before exporting the video?
Yes. After the translation step, the app opens a bilingual editor where you can view, edit each line, split or merge sentences, and preview directly on the video before exporting.
Voice AI
What do I need to clone a voice?
Just 1–3 audio samples of 3–10 seconds each, or record directly in the app. The cleaner the sample, the better the result — a built-in noise/music separation filter can clean it up before cloning. The sample language is auto-detected from 99 languages.
Can I create a new voice without any audio sample?
Yes, with the "Voice Design" tab: describe the voice you want (gender, age, pitch, style, accent…) and the AI generates a matching voice. Preview, refine the description, then save it as a reusable voice.
How do I move my cloned voices to another computer?
In the Library tab, select the voices and export them into a single .gxvoice file, then import that file on the other machine. This also works as a backup of your voice collection.
Can the generated speech express emotion?
Yes. You can insert nonverbal cues (laugh, sigh, surprise…) into the text, or click "Enhance with AI" to let AI add context-appropriate emotion. For Chinese and English you can even fine-tune per-word pronunciation.
Is my voice sample uploaded anywhere?
No. Cloning and synthesis run entirely on your machine; voice samples are stored locally only.
Technical
Can I use it on a computer without a GPU?
Yes. Orino Studio supports CPU mode for all regular computers. Processing will be slower than with a GPU, but all features remain fully functional.
How long does the initial setup take?
The Setup Wizard typically completes in 2–5 minutes depending on your internet speed. AI resources (speech recognition, translation, voice) are downloaded separately and only need to be set up once.
Does the app update automatically?
Yes. Go to Settings → "Check for updates" — when a new version is available, click "Install and restart" and the app updates itself. No manual installer download needed.
Output files are filling my disk. How do I clean up?
Go to Settings → Storage to see the total size of output files and clean them up per category: translated videos, generated voices, downloaded videos, recordings — no need to hunt for folders manually.
I encountered an error. Where do I get support?
You can reach us via Telegram or Zalo through the support buttons in the bottom-right corner of the website, or send a description of the error with a screenshot for the fastest support.
